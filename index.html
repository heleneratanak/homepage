About Me

Artist & Designer

Helene Ratanak is an artist and a designer who is earning her bachelor degree in Graphic Design and minoring in Mobile Application Development from University of Massachusetts Dartmouth. She wants to advance her skill in developing mobile apps after she found an interest in creating a visual prototype of product design / UX-UI. She also inspired by figure drawing, photography, and traveling.

Artist Statment

To utilize my skills, knowledge and education acquired through my experience as an art and design student, I enjoy creating visual concept in fine art and digital to communicate the ideas. One of my inspirations is art & design history which it pushes me to explore, investigate, and analyze the concept from famous artists.
